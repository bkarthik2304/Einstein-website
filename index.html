Albert Einstein

Theoretical physicist

I, Albert Einstein (born 1879), am a theoretical physicist best known for developing the theory of relativity and the famous equation E = mc². I make significant contributions to quantum mechanics and won the 1921 Nobel Prize in Physics for discovering the photoelectric effect. Born in Germany, I later become a Swiss and then an American citizen, playing a key role in alerting the U.S. to potential nuclear weapons research before World War II.
